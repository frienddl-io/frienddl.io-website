frienddl.io supports #BlackLivesMatter

Staying silent makes us complicit in institutionalized oppression.

If you see users on skribbl.io being racist or otherwise disrepectful about this important movement, please call it out and votekick.

Find out how you can donate your time, effort, or money here.

frienddl.io is free & not for profit

...and will always be. But if you want to show your support you can do so here.

frienddl.io is open-sourced

...and will always be. If you know how to code or interested in learning, definitely check out the known bugs & enhancements here.

frienddl.io is privacy-oriented

All data & logs stay on your machine.

frienddl.io is platform-agnostic

All versions are meant to have feature parity and a nearly identical codebase.

frienddl.io respects skribblio's rules

It's a simple tool to automate actions that can be done manually.

frienddl.io is not affliated with skribbl.io

This was developed independently, though some skribbl.io brand elements are utilized to match their theming.
